Privacy Policy

Solar SPV Analytics Platform  |  Effective Date: 17 May 2025  |  Last Updated: 17 May 2025

🔒 Short version: This app does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All data you upload stays on your device session only.

1. Who We Are

Solar SPV Analytics is a professional Operations & Maintenance (O&M) tool designed for solar power plant engineers and technicians. It enables on-site analysis of SMA inverter data without requiring a cloud database or user accounts.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Any uploaded inverter data files

3. How Your Data Is Handled

When you upload an Excel file (.xlsx / .xls) containing inverter data, the file is processed entirely within your current browser session using Streamlit's in-memory session state. The data:

  • Is never written to a database or persistent storage
  • Is never transmitted to any third-party server
  • Is automatically discarded when you close the app or refresh the page

4. Internet Connection

The app loads its user interface from our hosted web service at solar-analytics-app.streamlit.app. This connection is secured via HTTPS. Standard web server access logs (IP address, timestamp) may be retained by Streamlit Cloud's infrastructure for security purposes, subject to Streamlit's Privacy Policy.

5. Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party infrastructure:

  • Streamlit Cloud — Hosts the web application. See Streamlit Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts — Loads typography (Inter, Space Grotesk). Subject to Google Privacy Policy.

6. Children's Privacy

This application is intended for professional use by adults (O&M engineers and solar plant operators). It is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Security

All communication between the app and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. Since no personal data is stored, there is no risk of a personal data breach from our application.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the GitHub repository.
